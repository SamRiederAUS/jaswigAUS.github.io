Are standing desks good

Our bodies are not made to do something (whatever it is) for too long at a time. Our bodies are made to change positions and to move around as much as possible. The best position is always the next position. So change positions as many as possible. A standing desks makes you do just that.

How can a standing desk help

A standing desks makes the work environment a lot more dynamic. It makes sure that you move around more often. A sitting desks stimulates people to reside in a certain position for a very long time. Sitting makes people tired and thus enhances them to keep where they are. A standing desk draws attention and makes people choose to stand up and move. This is why someone should consider a standing desk. It makes it easier to find more balance between sitting and moving around.

  • Burn more calories Standing desks make people move around more and switch positions.
  • Take pressure from your spine because sitting exerts a lot of pressure on the discs present in the spine
  • Activate more and big muscle groupsstanding and moving around activates big muscle groups
  • Boost your blood flowbecause large muscle groups are activated
  • Makes you more efficientbecause blood flow increases which keeps you awake
